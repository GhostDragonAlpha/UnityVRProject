TROUBLESHOOTING

Q: Exports not showing in Inspector?

A: Should not break - property getters maintain compatibility.
   If issues occur, clear script cache: File > Clear Cache > Reload

A: Delete project.godot and reimport. Or manually reset values in Inspector.
